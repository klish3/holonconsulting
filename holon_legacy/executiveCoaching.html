BRIANKANYANGARARA

GENERALBIOGRAPHY

Brian has worked in corporate for over 30 years. During this time he has operated from the customer-facing frontline, to company Director Level, and has worked in Manufacturing, Business-to- industrial consumer marketing with AECI and Group 5, and in the financial services sector with Nedcor, where he rose to the level of Divisional Director. Brain has operated in South Africa, sub-Saharan Africa, the Far East (Hong Kong and Singapore) and in the Middle East.

Brian heads up Holon Management Consulting. A business consulting unit that focuses on enabling organizations improve operational efficiencies and bottom line profitability through the following:

  • Business Process Management improvement
  • Building strategies that work
  • Coaching executives to be effective in their organizations

The extensive experience gained over the years as well as the ability to run a successful management and business consulting company makes Brian a suitable as an executive coach and mentor because he calls on his substantial experience and exposure to be effective as a coach.

EDUCATION & TRAINING

Brian holds an MBA and has attended Business and Management schools such as WITS, Harvard, Insead and London Business School. A chemical engineer who moved into Financial Services Brian takes great interest in people and leadership development

COACHING WORK AND INTERNATIONAL EXPOSURE

Brian has coached over 20 senior leaders in executive and senior management positions across a wide variety of industries in South Africa and sub Saharan Africa and has received excellent praise from coachees, their bosses and colleagues on the positive impact he has been able to make in the lives of these executives as well as their organizations.

Brian has worked with managers and executives in the following companies: Hollard Insurance, Standard Bank, First National Bank, Zurich Insurance and Saint-Gobain.

COACHING APPROACH, STYLE AND PRACTISE

Coaching for effectiveness is Brian’s approach. Coachees who have worked with him attest to an approach that addresses the “real issues with real and practical solutions that are immediately usable”.

Clear outcomes are agreed at the beginning of the coaching and coaching occurs through conversation and practices that build new skills and habits. Each coaching engagement is unique and tailored to address the specific needs of the coachee.
